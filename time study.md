# Additives Time Study for Extruders 4 and 6

## Process
The current process of adding additives into the dosing unit of the extruders is as follows:  

1. Additives are weighed under the hoppers. The weight is recorded in the Additives Log Book as the starting weight.
2. Operator places the nozzles within the additives.
3. Operator calibrates the infeed control panels of the dosing units to activate the suction tubes for the calibrated amount of time.
4. Operator calibrates the dosing percentage.
5. Dosing unit is activated.
6. Operator uses the shovel to move the additives to fill in the caved-in section of the additives.
7. Once four-fifths of a box has been produced, a sample is then sent to the lab to test whether the products meet the customer specifications.
8. Based on lab comments, the dosing percentage is adjusted.
9. Once the job is completed, the additives are weighed again under the hoppers. The weight is recorded in the Additives Log Book as the end weight.
